Keynote Speaker

Dr. Kate Starbird

We are excited to announce Dr. Kate Starbird, associate Professor at the Department of Human Centered Design & Engineering (HCDE), as our keynote speaker.

Dr. Starbird's research explores the intersection of technology and society focusing on online social platforms' potential for collaboration and addressing concerns about abuse, manipulation, and trust erosion. She broadly studies Human-Computer Interactions (HCI), Information-Communication Technologies (ICTs), and user's social media behavior during natural and man made crises. Currently, Dr. Starbird research focus shifted on the spread of disinformation within the context of her area.

Additionally, She serves as adjunct faculty in the Paul G. Allen School of Computer Science & Engineering and the Information School;she is a data science fellow at the eScience Institute and the director of the 'Emerging Capacities of Mass Participation (emCOMP)'. Dr. Starbird is also a co-founder of the University of Washington's 'Center for an Informed Public'.

For more information about Dr. Kate Starbird, checkout the profile page on the University of Washington Website.
